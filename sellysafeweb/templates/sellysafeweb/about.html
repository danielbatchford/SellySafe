About

SellySafe aims to let UOB students view reports of incidents occurring around Selly Oak. Is someone walking through your garden? Is someone trying to open doors on your street? Report it on the map and let others know where and when it happened, allowing them to take necessary precautions with regards to the incident. This site gives you an interactive map, letting you see recent incidents near you.

mapGo To The Map

Why

This was created as a lockdown project to help people stay safe in and around Selly Oak. I aim to help improve safety for students living in post first year accommodation. Furthermore, I aim to give first year students an indication of the safer areas to live in 2nd year, as well as where to avoid when going out.

Disclaimer

While I hope this map helps people during their time in Selly Oak, this is not intended to be a replacement for the Official Police Crime Map for Selly Oak, which contains recent reported incidents and the police investigation status for each incident.
